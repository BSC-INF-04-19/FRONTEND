ABOUT US

Malawi Housing corporation was established in 1970 by Dr hastings kamuzu banda with the purpose of constructing cheap town houses.
And also he wanted townships to have beautiful houses for the citizens of the country. Malawi housing corporation is a government
parastatal which accomodates citizens with good quality houses. And we have houses in each and every district, for example in Blantyre
We have many areas in townships same as Zomba , Lilongwe and Mzuzu.
